Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

1. Information Collection And Use

The 3-in-1 Utility App operates entirely on your local device. We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). All BMI and Calculator inputs are processed locally on your phone and are never transmitted to any external servers.

2. Local Storage

This app uses your device's Local Storage strictly to save your UI preferences (e.g., Dark Mode settings). This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services (Exchange Rates)

To provide live currency conversion, this app connects to a third-party Exchange Rate API. When you use the Currency Converter, your device makes a secure request to fetch the latest rates. No personal data is sent during this request.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact Prathamesh Sathe.
