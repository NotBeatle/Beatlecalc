    Privacy Policy

    Your privacy is our absolute priority. Beatle Calc is designed as a completely offline utility application.

    1. Zero Data Collection

    We do not collect, track, store, or transmit any personal or identifiable information. There are no analytics, trackers, or background monitoring scripts included in this software.

    2. 100% Offline Operation

    Beatle Calc makes zero external network requests. All functions rely strictly on hardcoded databases packed directly into the app. Note: Because the app cannot connect to the internet, currency exchange rates are static historical records intended for demonstration, not financial use.

    3. Local Device Storage

    Any data you generate (such as calculation history or Dark Mode preferences) is saved directly to your device's isolated local storage. This data never leaves your phone and is automatically cleared if you uninstall the app or clear its cache.
